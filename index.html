Independent Coffee Quality Evaluation & Certification Advisory

I provide independent consulting services for coffee growers, traders, and exporters, focusing on coffee quality evaluation, grading, and sustainability compliance. My work involves on-ground assessment of coffee lots, identifying defects, analysing grade percentages, and offering clear insights to support better decision-making. I work closely with estates, traders, and exporters to improve coffee quality, consistency, and overall market readiness. This includes evaluating post-harvest practices, drying methods, and processing systems, and suggesting practical improvements that can enhance both quality and value. I also support clients in aligning with sustainability and certification requirements, including organic, Rainforest Alliance, and 4C standards. My approach is practical, field-oriented, and focused on improving traceability, ensuring compliance, and strengthening market confidence.
